# 📊 Sales Performance Analysis – 2024

## Project Overview
This project analyzes 12 months of sales data for a fictional electronics retailer. The goal is to uncover revenue trends, identify top-performing products, and evaluate regional performance to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Key Questions Answered
- What is the total revenue and overall profit margin for 2024?
- Which months had the highest and lowest sales?
- Which regions are generating the most revenue?
- What are the top-performing products by sales and units sold?
- How does sales growth trend month over month?

## 📂 Dataset
- **Source:** Sample/simulated dataset
- **Records:** 240 rows (12 months × 4 regions × 5 products)
- **Fields:** Order ID, Month, Region, Product, Units Sold, Unit Price, Total Sales, Profit Margin

---

## 📈 Key Findings

### Revenue
- Total annual revenue exceeded **$10M** across all regions and products
- Monthly revenue showed consistent performance with peaks in Q4

### Top Products
- **Laptops** generated the highest revenue due to high unit price
- **Keyboards** had the highest profit margins at 35%

### Regional Insights
- All four regions (North, South, East, West) contributed fairly evenly
- Regional breakdowns reveal opportunities for targeted marketing

### Profit
- Average profit margin across all products: **~26%**
- Keyboards and Smartphones were the most profitable product lines
